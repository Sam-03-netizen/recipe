Delicious Fried chicken

Ingredients

  • Chicken pieces (drumsticks, thighs, or wings)
  • Buttermilk
  • All-purpose flour
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Paprika
  • Garlic powder
  • Onion powder
  • Cayenne pepper (optional for heat)
  • Oil for frying (e.g., vegetable or canola oil)

Recipe

  1. Place chicken pieces in a bowl and cover with buttermilk. Marinate in the refrigerator for at least 1 hour (or overnight for best results).
  2. In a separate bowl, mix the flour with salt, pepper, paprika, garlic powder, onion powder, and cayenne pepper.
  3. Remove the chicken from the buttermilk and let the excess drip off.
  4. Coat each piece in the seasoned flour mixture, pressing it on to ensure it sticks well.
  5. Heat oil in a deep skillet or fryer to 350째F (175째C).
  6. Fry chicken in batches, turning occasionally, for 12 to 15 minutes or until golden brown and cooked through (internal temperature should reach 165째F / 75째C).
  7. Remove and drain on paper towels.
  8. Let it rest for a few minutes, then serve hot!
